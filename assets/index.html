About Us

BINUS SCHOOL Serpong, a proud member of the BINA NUSANTARA family, is dedicated to fostering and empowering students to excel academically and personally. Our state-of-the-art campus in Serpong, Tangerang, offers a nurturing environment where learners can thrive.

We strive to provide a world-class education that prepares students for global challenges. Our mission is to shape future leaders by equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to succeed in a rapidly evolving world.
